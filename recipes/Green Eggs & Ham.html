Green Eggs & Ham

Try them, try them, and you may! Try them and you may, I say.

Ingredients

  • 6 extra large free range eggs
  • 120g ham off the bone
  • 50ml green food colouring

Directions

  1. Place a drop of food colouring on a slice of ham. Spread it around with your finger or a basting brush and pat any excess dye off with a paper towel. Fry the ham until the edges turn crispy (optional).
  2. Combine water and food dye in a small bowl. Crack an egg and let just the yolk bathe in the mixture.
  3. Heat a frying pan on low and add a teaspoon of oil. Pour the egg whites into the pan one at a time and let sit for a moment.
  4. Add the green yolk to the middle of the sizzling egg white and cook to your liking.
  5. Remove eggs carefully and place on plate. Place ham on plate and season accordingly.
